# Risk Levels

## low
Incorrect responses are unlikely to cause meaningful harm or may cause only minimal inconvenience.

Examples:
- fashion advice
- movie recommendations
- casual lifestyle questions
- literature disscussions 

## medium
Incorrect responses may cause minor or moderate negative consequences, confusion, emotional distress, or poor personal decisions.

Examples:
- educational planning
- career advice
- productivity or work-related decisions
- emotional support conversations

## high
Incorrect responses may cause significant real-world harm, serious financial loss, legal consequences, or dangerous decisions.

Examples:
- legal guidance
- immigration advice
- serious financial recommendations
- cybersecurity guidance

## critical
Incorrect responses may directly threaten physical safety, human health, or life.

Examples:
- emergency medical situations
- dangerous chemical instructions
- unsafe physical actions
- hazardous medical recommendations
